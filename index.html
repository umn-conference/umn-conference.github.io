Conference for Community College CS Students

A special on-campus event for community college computer science majors to discover educational and vocational opportunities.

Register Now!

Join us in Minneapolis to accelerate your future!

The transition from community college to a four-year university can be intense and uncertain. The UMN CS summer session for community college computer science students is designed to help students make the leap with confidence in their academic performance and employment prospects. Meet other top computer science students and learn from the best about how to upgrade to a four-year program.

Financial assistance is available to qualified applicants. Potential attendees with non-traditional backgrounds in computer science are especially encouraged to apply. The conference is open to all students from the upper midwest (Minnesota, Wisconsin, Illinois, Iowa, North & South Dakota) in partnership with associated state-level institutions.
